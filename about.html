Salama Pamoja Conference 2022 Logo

The 2022 Salama Pamoja Logo was decided by various activists and creatives committed towards providing a safe environment for everyone. After several suggestions, sampling was done and the final logo chosen based on how inclusive and uniting it was towards the victims of GBV and sexual abuse.
